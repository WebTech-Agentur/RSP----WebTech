About Us

Our Company History & Mission

At RSP, we look back on a proud history of supplying high-quality natural stone products. Our mission is to provide reliable and customized natural stone solutions that meet the demands of construction and design projects throughout Germany and beyond.

1500 m² of natural stone delivered annually
5 quarries owned by RSP
10000 tons of raw stone processed annually
2000 projects successfully completed

Your Source for Natural Stone Excellence

RSP supplies high-quality natural stone for a wide variety of construction projects. From elegant interior flooring to durable outdoor terraces, we offer customized solutions – including kitchen countertops and fireplace surrounds – that are tailored exactly to your specifications. We specialize in the reliable delivery of natural stone in large quantities, focusing primarily on importing to Germany.
